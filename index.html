Our Chef: Georgia Reign


Georgia was born in Charlotte, NC. He was raised in Boston, MA and attended the New England Culinary Institute.

If he’s not at Bistros, you’ll find him cheering on the Boston Red Sox or spending time with his dog, Meka. He considers Julia Child his hero and his mother to be his greatest inspiration.

Georgia been working in the restaurant industry for a very long time and really enjoys the environment Bistros offers.
